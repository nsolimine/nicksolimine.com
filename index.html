About

Front End Web Developer,
Audio engineer, Musician

I have always been a creative person. For as long as I can remember, I've always spent my time creating by using my mind and my hands to convey what I see in the world around me. I've been a musician for half of my life and learning to express myself through the music I love has been the most rewarding journey I've ever taken. In 2007 I enrolled in the University of Cincinnati's College Conservatory of Music to study in the Electronic Media Program. I wanted to learn sound design, and what goes into creating music- from recording instruments in the studio, to post-production, to having a finished product that artists could be proud of, and a product that I could be proud of for having brought to life from start to finish. I'll never stop creating, and this drive has lead me to finding my joy in a new creative medium- world of Web Development.
